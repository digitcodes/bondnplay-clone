About BondnPlay

Our Mission

BondnPlay is a hybrid platform offering virtual and in-person family game nights. Designed to strengthen family bonds, build teamwork, and create fun, competitive environments where families connect with others across North America.

Services Offered

  • Online Family Game Nights (via Zoom & game platforms)
  • In-Person Game Events (seasonal, indoor venues)
  • Family Tournaments with Prizes
  • Extended Family & Group Options
  • Private Game Night Bookings for special occasions
  • Team-Building packages for schools or community groups

Target Market

  • Families with children aged 6+
  • Parents seeking screen-free bonding alternatives
  • Grandparents & extended families
  • Homeschooling or faith-based families
  • Community organizations looking for family-friendly events
